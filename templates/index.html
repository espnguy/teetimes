📅 Add Tee Time Watch

ℹ️ How it works

  1. Enter your ForeUp course URL from the booking page
  2. Set your target date and preferred time window
  3. Click "Start Watching" — the server polls every 2 min
  4. When a tee time appears, the job turns green
  5. Click "View & Confirm" to see options and book
Tip: ForeUp releases tee times exactly 7 days in advance at midnight. Start a job the day before to be ready the moment they drop!
