ℹ️ How it works

  1. Enter your booking URL — ForeUp or GolfNow/TeeItUp
  2. Set your target date and preferred time window
  3. Click "Start Watching" — the server polls every 2 min
  4. When a tee time appears, the job turns green
  5. Tap the Pushover notification or click "Book on [Platform]" to open the booking page
Tip: Most courses release tee times 7–14 days in advance. Check your course's booking rules. Start a job the evening before to be ready the moment they drop!
